First-Come, First-Served (FCFS) is the simplest scheduling algorithm. Processes are assigned to the CPU in the order they arrive. It is non-preemptive, meaning once a process has the CPU, it runs to completion.

**Key Formula:**
Turnaround Time = Completion Time - Arrival Time
